About Sector Arcade

🕹️ Free Browser Games for Everyone

Our Mission

Sector Arcade exists for one reason: to make great arcade games instantly accessible to everyone, everywhere, completely free. No downloads. No signups. No paywalls. No friction. Just open your browser, click, and play.

We believe the best gaming experiences shouldn't be locked behind installs, accounts, or subscriptions. The golden age of arcade games was defined by accessibility — put a coin in, start playing. We've brought that spirit to the browser.

Our Games

Every game on Sector Arcade is built with the same philosophy: immediate fun, genuine depth, and endless replayability. Our current lineup includes:

  • Sector Arkanoid — A neon-powered brick breaker with power-ups and satisfying physics.
  • Galaxy Assault — An intense space shooter against relentless alien waves.
  • Void Bird — A one-tap challenge set in a neon void dimension.
  • Castle Destroyer — Physics-based siege action with realistic projectile physics.
  • Neon Blocks — The classic falling blocks puzzle with a neon glow-up.
  • Serpent Ultra — The classic snake game supercharged with power-ups.
  • Sector Chess — Full chess vs AI (3 levels) or online PvP with room codes.
  • Sector Backgammon — Complete backgammon with doubling cube, AI, and online PvP.

New games are added regularly. Check the Coming Soon section for what's in development.

Why Browser Games?

Browser games represent the most democratic form of gaming. They work on any device with a modern browser — a high-end gaming PC, an old school laptop, a smartphone, a library computer. They require no storage space, no updates to manage, and no accounts to maintain.

For us, this accessibility isn't just a technical feature — it's the entire point. Gaming should be for everyone, not just those with the latest hardware or the budget for premium games.

The Technology

All Sector Arcade games are built with modern web technologies — HTML5, JavaScript, and CSS. They use WebGL for rendering where appropriate, run entirely client-side after initial load, and are designed to work on any modern browser without plugins or special requirements.

The site itself is hosted on GitHub Pages — a reliable, fast, globally-distributed platform that ensures games load quickly from anywhere in the world.
