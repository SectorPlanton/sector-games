Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sector Arcade ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our website at www.sector-games.com.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, and game interactions collected automatically via analytics tools.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and IP address.
  • Cookies: Small data files placed on your device to improve your experience and serve relevant advertisements.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the website and games
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense and similar services
  • To analyze traffic and usage patterns via Google Analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google may use cookies and other tracking technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services including:

  • Google Analytics – for website analytics
  • Google AdSense – for advertising
  • GitHub Pages – for website hosting

6. Cookies

We use cookies for analytics and advertising. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality. See our Cookie Policy for more details.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us via the Contact page to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact page.
