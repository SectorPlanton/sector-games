Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

How We Use Cookies

Sector Arcade uses cookies for the following purposes:

Cookie Type Purpose Provider
Analytics Track page visits and user behaviour to improve the site Google Analytics
Advertising Display relevant ads based on interests Google AdSense / Ad Networks
Functional Remember preferences such as high scores First-party (localStorage)

Third-Party Cookies

Our ad partners (including Google AdSense) use cookies to serve ads based on your interests. These cookies may track your browsing across websites. You can manage Google ad preferences at adssettings.google.com.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Note: Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the site, including game score saving.

More Information

For more information about how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy. Questions? Contact us via the Contact page.
