🎮 About the Game

Galaxy Assault is an intense top-down space shooter that pits you against endless waves of alien forces threatening the galaxy. Inspired by the golden age of arcade shooters, it delivers the fast-paced action of classic games like Space Invaders and Galaga while adding modern touches that keep the experience fresh and progressively challenging.

You pilot a lone fighter ship — the last line of defense against a relentless alien armada. Each wave brings more enemies, faster projectiles, and increasingly aggressive formation patterns. The game tests not just your reflexes but your ability to prioritize threats, conserve your ship, and maximize your firepower when openings appear.

The upgrade system is one of Galaxy Assault's standout features. As you destroy enemies and progress through waves, you collect power-ups that enhance your weapons — spread shot, rapid fire, shields, and screen-clearing missiles all become available. Building the right combination for your playstyle is half the fun.

The visual design leans into the drama of space combat: neon laser fire, colorful explosions, enemy formations silhouetted against a starfield, and your ship glowing with each upgrade. Galaxy Assault is proof that the space shooter genre never gets old — it just keeps getting better.

🎯 How to Play

Move your ship using arrow keys or WASD. On mobile, use the on-screen joystick or tilt controls. Your ship shoots automatically — just focus on positioning and survival.

Enemies appear in formation at the top of the screen and move in patterns, occasionally breaking formation to dive at your ship. Shoot enemies before they reach you or fire projectiles.

Collect power-ups dropped by destroyed enemies. Survive as many waves as possible — the game ends when your ship is destroyed and you have no lives remaining.

💡 Tips & Tricks

  • Stay in constant motion. A stationary ship is an easy target — keep weaving.
  • Focus fire on enemy groups approaching low on screen before isolated high enemies.
  • The shield power-up is your most valuable asset. Save it for when enemies are clustering dangerously close.
  • Spread shot clears multiple columns at once — ideal for wave starts when enemies are tightly grouped.
  • Learn enemy patterns — most have predictable movements that create safe zones to exploit.
  • The missile power-up is best saved for boss encounters, not regular waves.
